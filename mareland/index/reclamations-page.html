About MaReLand Reclamations

Welcome to Reclamations: The MaReLand Blog!

“Reclamations” is a blog facilitated by the MaReLand project.
The website sets to be a free networking platform bringing together researchers, activists, and sea aficionados interested in land reclamation processes. It is a place where everybody can share stories from the field, ideas, findings, perspectives, and ongoing activities on related topics in Malaysia, the broader Asian region, and beyond. The Reclamations blog also provides a space where to quickly publish beyond academic venues.

For further information and details on how to submit a post, please contact the editor at pierpa.degios@zoho.com or pierpaolo.degiosa@upol.cz.
